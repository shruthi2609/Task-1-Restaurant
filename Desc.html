ROASTED STUFFED MUSHROOMS

Hearty comfort meets healthy eating in
this delicious recipe. Plus it's kind
to your wallet as well as your waistline.

  • Ingredients
  1. 8 large (about 650g) flat mushrooms
  2. Olive oil spray
  3. 1 brown onion, finely chopped
  4. 2 garlic cloves, crushed

BAKED SPRING ROLLS

Awaken the senses with these fast and fresh authentic
Vietnamese spring rolls.

  • Ingredients
  1. 80g cellophane (mung bean) noodles
  2. 5 dried shiitake mushrooms
  3. 225g can water chestnuts, drained, rinsed, finely chopped
  4. 2 cups finely shredded wombok (Chinese cabbage)
  5. 2 carrots, peeled, coarsely grated
  6. 65g (1 cup) fresh bean sprouts, ends trimmed
